INVESTMENTS AND FUNDING

Startups supported by GUSEC TIDE Scheme can leverage the following financial assistance. Idea Validation – Up to ₹4 lakh in support Validation and development of an idea through Entrepreneur-in-Residence (TIDE EiR) Funding support of up to ₹4 lakh per idea / startup A startup needs to be at early-idea or idea stage stage The funding shall be utilized towards validation and developing Proof-of-Concept of an idea (POC) during the stipulated timeline Prototype Development – Up to ₹7 lakh in support Prototype development grant for TIDE supported startups through GUSEC Funding support of up to ₹7 lakh per idea / startup A startup needs to be ready with Proof-of-Concept of the idea The funding shall be utilized towards validation and developing Proof-of-Concept of an idea (POC) during the stipulated timeline
